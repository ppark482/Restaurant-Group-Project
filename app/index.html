Founded by Monsieur Park and le Chef Griffeth, Le Cochon de Fer was opened in 2010.

The idea came about when Monsieru Paul, from Big Major Cay island, was becoming over-populated with his sounder of swine that he decided the best way to control his pig population was to make his overabundance available to the people of the mainland. With the help of his good friend and now business partner chef Griffeth, they created a seasonally exquisite menu filled with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, spices and of course, pork. The result is food that is honest, straightforward and pays homage to the bounty of Major Cay Island.

The restaurant became a huge hit from the beginning, with eager mainlanders sometimes waiting hours for an opportunity to experience Le cochon de fer with its warm hues, soft lighting and relaxed ambience that invited guests to settle in, get comfortable, and have a great time.

The le Cochon de Fer experience is about more than just food.
